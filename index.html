GloveBud

GloveBud is a gesture control system that captures the input gesture by the user and enables control of a Smart Home. The Glove is equipped with flex sensors and an accelerometer to predict the gesture input by the user. The Smart Home is Raspberry Pi powered. GloveBud is aimed to help the physically disabled and old age people.

Internet of Things, Home Automation

Bella

Bella is an assistant that is capable of Natural Language Processing and has an AI written from scratch. Bella runs on an Android Smart Home, can take input commands from the user in form of speech and perform the task given. Bella is also capable of controlling a Smart Home that consists of a Smart Room, Smart Kitchen and Smart Garden. Bella is targetted as a cheaper alternative to Google Home and Amazon's Alexa without compromising quality.

Personal Assistant, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Langauge Processing, Internet of Things,Home Automation

GARB-e

GARB-e is an IoT System consisting of Smart TrashCans and Autonomous robots that constantly monitor the garbage levels in a city and autnomously collect and dispose of the garbage. The bots are TensorFlow (Google's Neural Network) enabled that helps to segregate the waste. The bots move across different smart bins using shortest path alogirthms.

Internet of Things, Machine Learning, TensorFlow, Autnomous
